Casting votes from Android

Step 1a

Login to your @mcc.edu.in account on your phone

How do I do that?
  • Open any Google App
  • Tap on your profile picture to open the account switcher
  • Tap on Add Account
  • Login to your @mcc.edu.in account
I don't know my password!
  • Never reset it before? Ask your class rep for the default password
  • Reset it but forgot? Use the Forgot password option

Step 1b

Login to your @mcc.edu.in account in your browser

Chrome?
  • Open google.com on Chrome
  • Tap on your profile picture on the top right
  • No @mcc.edu.in ID? Tap on Add accounts and add it
Something else?
  • Open google.com on your browser
  • Tap on your profile picture on the top right
  • If your @mcc.edu.in ID isn't there, tap on Add accounts and add it

When your professor calls your name for attendance:

  • Turn on camera and mic
  • Show your face and say present

  • Or if you can, ask someone to hold the phone and use the backcamera
